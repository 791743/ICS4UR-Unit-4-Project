# ICS4UR-Unit-4-Project

Introduction:
Aliens living on exoplanets are looking for gold to collect to bring back to thier homelands! Although, this is not easy! There are harsh meteor showers, poisonous gases, and UFO's on the lookout! They must collect the coins as quickly as they can so they can go back home safely, and use their materials wisely to survive!

Game Features:
- Player can move an alien character around a planet to collect coins while avoidiing harmful enemies.
- You can progress through three levels where the enemies have different movements and speeds.
- You can use your coins to purchase boosts in between levels.
- You can restart the game in between the games.

How to run the program:
Open the Escape The Exoplanet code on CodeHS on a JavaSwing file. Ensure all the files are imported and the GameFrame file is the main file and click Run. Once you save your credientals are saved, click play. Use arrow keys to move the character and in the case it does not work in the first few seconds, double click your game screen! Move the character to the coins to collect and make sure the enemies do not touch your character or you will die. Level is completed when you collect all coins and resets every time you die. Coins reset when you restart the game.
